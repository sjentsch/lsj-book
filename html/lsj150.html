7What’s the diﬀerence between treatment and simple contrasts, I hear you ask? Well, as a basic example consider a gender main eﬀect, with m=0 and f=1. The coeﬃcient corresponding to the treatment contrast will measure the diﬀerence in mean between females and males, and the intercept would be the mean of the males. However, with a simple contrast, i.e., m=-1 and f=1, the intercept is the average of the means and the main eﬀect is the diﬀerence of each group mean from the intercept.
